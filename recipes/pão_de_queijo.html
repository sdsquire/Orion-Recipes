Pão de Queijo Recipe

Description

This is a description about the pão de quejio

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • ⅔ cup Milk
  • 1½ cup Tapioca Flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 400⁰F
  2. In a blender, add milk, olive oil and egg. Blend to combine.
  3. Add flour, salt, and cheese in blender
  4. Blend ingredients, scraping down sides of blender if needed.
  5. Pour mixture into greased mini muffin tins, stopping ⅛″ from the top of tin.
  6. Bake for 13-15 minutes, or until golden brown.
